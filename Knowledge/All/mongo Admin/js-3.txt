1. preventDefault vs. stopPropagation vs. stopImmediatePropagation
Ans:-
    a. preventDefault: Cancels the event if it is cancelable, 
                        without stopping further propagation of the event.

    b. stopPropagation: Prevents further propagation of the current event.

    c. stopImmediatePropagation: Prevents other listeners of the same event 
                                from being called.
